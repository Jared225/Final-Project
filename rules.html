Black Jack Rules

  • Draw:
    • To draw a card you press the draw card button located at the top of the page.
    • This version of Black Jack allows you to draw as many as 8 times until you are either satisfied with your score or your score is over 21
  • Keep Score:
    • Keep Score allows you to keep your score that you curently have and will display a text box alert on what your final score is.
    • Use this button if you do not feel you can make a score of 21.
    • Example: you draw a Queen of clubs and a King of hearts and the only card that will give you 21 is an ace. Thus you choose to keep you current score without taking a risk by pressing the "Keep Score" button.
  • Reset:
    • The Reset button allows you to reset the score and the deck.
    • The Deck is also reset to a random order again so you don't have to worry about repeats.
  • Your Value:
    • The Value shows you the value of your card so you don't need to worry about making sure your score is right.
    • The Value will change every time you draw a new card.
  • Your Score:
    • The score is the total value of all your cards you have drawn in that current game.
    • Use this to keep track of what your total score is in the game to know if you want to keep your hand or take a risk.
  • To Win:
    • To Win you need to have a score of 21.
    • When you score 21, an alert text box will display that you have scored 21!!!
    • When you keep your score you do not win, but you do not lose and you will also see an alert text box with your score
  • To Lose:
    • To Lose you need to have a scoe that is larger than 21.
    • When you score larger than 21, an alert text box will display that you have busted!!!
  • Card Value:
    • Ace: 1
    • Two: 2
    • Three: 3

    • Eight: 8
    • Nine: 9
    • Ten: 10
    • Queen: 10
    • King: 10

  • Number of Each Card:
    • There are 4 cards for each type.
  • Type of Cards:
    • There are 4 types of cards
      • Diamond
      • Heart
      • Club
      • Spade
  • Total Number of Cards:
    • 52 Cards in total.
